Pancake

servings

20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before frosting or serving.
